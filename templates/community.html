Seen in the Lojban Community

This section summarises the latest goings on in Lojbanistan. It should aggregate Lojban-related feeds including the LiveJournal community and other weblogs. The sidebar can contain the most recent mailing list subjects and other topical data (e.g. latest del.icio.us bookmarks tagged with 'lojban' and new Google hits).

The Lojbanic community is not really active enough to generate regular del.icio.us bookmarks etc, perhaps we can do something to foster growth in this manner? I think the website redesign itself might do a bit to improve this, as we can show people how to get their new material linked from the main site automatically and that could encourage people to post more, or at least be more visible about it.
